02 SOMETHING SPECIAL

About Kore

Our Philosophy

Kore Productions' philosophy can be summarized in one sentence: High Quality video production at an unbeatable delivery timeline for a fair price. Why wait 5 to 6 working days when you can get your first draft in 24h ?

We offer a unique boutique design experience, where your ideas are brought to life in a creative, collaborative and fun environment.

The goal is to make a corporate video that is visually impressive and business-effective.

On-Demand Creative Services

Filling the gap between cheap, poorly-made video and high-end visual statements, Kore Productions’ teams can be deployed for any business in multiple formats.

The Kore team is supported by a talented pool of experienced cameramen, writers, actors and crew to maintain professional production values.

The options are limitless; tailoring our solutions to your outstanding project gives us a lot of flexibility. Simply put, we adapt to your project.

As Corporate videos experts, we will be honored to give your customized projects unmatched power, impact and quality.
